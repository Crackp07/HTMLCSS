Linear Equations

linear equation is an equation that may be put in the form

ax + b = 0

where a and b are known real numbers, called equation coefficients. We are looking for such a value, or such values, for the variable x, for which this equation will be fulfilled - the left side of equality will be equal to the right one.

Qadrtic Equations

The standard form of a quadratic equation looks like this:

ax2 + bx + c = 0

where a, b, c are known values and x is variable or unknown. Also, a can`t be 0
